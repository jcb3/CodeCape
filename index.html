About Marquette Technology Institute

Marquette Technology Institute (MTi) is a regional initiative by the Marquette Tech District Foundation, a non-profit corporation and Codefi, a coworking business center and anchor of the district, providing technical education, competitions and networking activities to develop, attract, and retain technology talent in southeast Missouri.

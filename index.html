Welcome to CodeCape

Helping to further programming education and technology in Southeast Missouri

What We Do

CodeCape offers access to education, mentorship and community in the areas of software development and technology. Whether you are looking for youth programs, adult programs, or a tech professional wanting to learn something new.

Youth Education

Children are growing up in a world immersed in technology, give them a head start with our youth programs.

Adult Education

The need for software developers is growing. Learn a new skill and find a new career with our adult programs.

Tech Professional

Youth Education

Introductory and intermediate programs for youth and young adults that teach basic programming and problem solving skills that will benefit them no matter what career they choose.

Upcoming Programs

Code for Cape Hackathon

July 22 - 24

This civic hackathon is an event where developers, designers, students, researchers, and civic leaders come together to design and develop tangible solutions to the problems we face daily in the towns we live. Teams with the best solutions win prizes and are eligible for service contracts to fully implement their technology solutions.
